Its really hard to introduce oneself. We keep on thinking what to write and what not.

I have started my career in teaching at Father Agnel School from 1996. I taught there for two and half years and then joined Lady Irwin Sr. Secondary School. I taught Grade XI and XII Computer Science for two years in Lady Irwin Sr. Secondary School and then shifted to Modern School, Barakhamba Road.

I learnt a lot under the guidance of Mr. Goutam Sarkar who is a HOD at Modern School. Worked with him for almost eight years and learnt that teacher is not about teaching a particular subject. With a word teacher, a lot many more responsibilities comes along. A teacher is a mentor, a guide, a friend with whom the student can talk, learn from, listen to, share with, etc. A real teacher is the one who can actually teach at the level of the student so that learning becomes easy. The main objective of a teacher is to make the learning happens in a joyful manner. All this has been taught by Mr. Sarkar who is actually a mentor for me.

In this journey of being a teacher, I met Mr. Mukesh Kumar, HOD DPS R. K. Puram. He helped me further to become what I am today.

I joined Ambience Public School in April 2008 and working as Head of Computer Department since then. I have over twenty years of experience in the field of education; teaching Computer Science, Informatics Practices and Multimedia & Web Technology to Grades XI and XII; and has been multifold responsibilities at my school (Result In-charge, ERP, Website, Almanac, Magazine).

I am a passionate learner and committed educator consistently working to make learning enjoyable for the learners. My foremost classroom pedagogy is centred around project-based learning. I have conducted ICT and Net safety workshops for all stakeholders.

I have been part of the curriculum development team for classes IX and X Information and Communication Technology, NCERT. I have co-authored class IX and X ICT for NCERT. I also also co-authored Class XII Computer Science and Informatics Practices, NCERT.

I have co-authored the Artificial Intelligence books for Grades VIII to X for BPB Publicaitons.

I have authored the Computer Science Sample Question Bank for Grade XII as per CBSE curriculum.

I have conducted Teacher Training workshops for SCERT Teachers.

I believe to be very positive about every aspect of life.
